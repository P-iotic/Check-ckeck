Help & FAQ

Quick Tips

  • Cart: Click ADD TO CART on a product page. See the item count in the top-right cart icon.
  • Filtering: Combine category filter, search, and the sort dropdown for best results.
  • Account: Register with a valid email; we store info locally on your device (no server).

How do I add products to the cart?

Open a product, choose a quantity, and click ADD TO CART. A toast will confirm, and the cart icon will bump.

How do I sort or search products?

On the home page, use the search box and the Sort dropdown (Price or Name). Sorting respects your current category filter and search term.

How do I create an account or login?

Go to Account, switch to Register, and complete the form. Passwords must be at least 6 characters. Use a valid email format (e.g., name@example.com).

How do I remove items from the cart?

Open the cart and click Remove next to the item, or decrease quantity to 0 (the item will be removed automatically).
